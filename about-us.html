Who We Are
Crafting
Journeys
Since 2009

Yatra Express began as a small passion project by a group of travel-obsessed friends who were tired of cookie-cutter tour packages. We wanted something more — raw, authentic, deeply personal encounters with the real India.

Today, we are a team of 60+ travel experts, local guides, and experience architects spread across 14 Indian cities. Every itinerary we create is handcrafted, every destination vetted by boots on the ground, and every traveller treated like a lifelong friend.

We do not just book trips — we design experiences that stay with you forever.

What Drives Us
Our Core
Values

Every decision we make, every destination we recommend, and every guide we train is grounded in these principles.

01
Authentic
Experiences

No tourist traps. No manufactured moments. We go where the locals go, eat what the locals eat, and sleep where the stories are told under starlit skies.

02
Community
First

Tourism should lift communities, not just enrich corporations. We work exclusively with local guides, family-run stays, and village artisans who know their land best.

03
Unhurried
Travel

India cannot be rushed. We build itineraries that breathe — with room for detours, chai breaks, and conversations that change your perspective on the world.

04
Radical
Transparency

No hidden fees, no vague itineraries, no bait-and-switch. Every rupee is accounted for and every detail is confirmed long before you pack your bag.

05
Responsible
Tourism

We offset carbon on every trip, promote zero-plastic trails, and donate 2% of every booking to conservation efforts across India's fragile ecosystems.

06
Obsessive
Quality

From the thread count of your sheets to the timing of a sunset boat ride — every detail is obsessed over, tested, and refined until it is just right.

Our Journey
How We Got
Here
2009
The First Trip

Four friends, a Tata Sumo, and a handwritten map of Rajasthan. That road trip sparked the idea that real India needed a different kind of travel company.

2012
Officially Founded

Yatra Express was registered in Mumbai with a team of 6, an office the size of a broom closet, and 12 hand-picked itineraries. All 12 sold out in year one.

2016
10,000 Travellers

We crossed our first major milestone — 10,000 happy travellers — and expanded operations to Jaipur, Kochi, and Varanasi, embedding ourselves deeper into local communities.

2020
Going Digital

Amid the pandemic, we pivoted fast — launching virtual India tours, local artisan marketplaces, and the foundation for this very platform you're browsing today.

2024
48,000+ Journeys

Today we operate across 14 cities, partner with 800+ local hosts, and have been featured in Condé Nast Traveller India and National Geographic as one of India's most trusted travel companies.
