Quick Answers
Frequently Asked
Questions
How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?
Our travel specialists respond to all enquiries within 4 hours during business hours (9am–7pm IST, Mon–Sat). For urgent queries, WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us — we typically reply within 30 minutes.
Can you build a fully custom itinerary for me?
Absolutely. Every itinerary we create is 100% custom — we do not use cookie-cutter packages. Share your interests, budget, dates, and group size and we will design a trip that fits you perfectly, from hotels to activities to transfers.
Do you handle visa assistance for India travel?
Yes — we provide complete visa guidance including e-Visa application support, documentation checklists, and embassy liaison for all nationalities. We also handle inner line permits for restricted areas like Ladakh, Spiti, and the Northeast.
What is your cancellation and refund policy?
Cancellations made 30+ days before travel receive a full refund minus processing fees. 15–30 days: 50% refund. Under 15 days: non-refundable. We strongly recommend travel insurance, which we can arrange for you.
Do you offer group tour packages for corporates?
Yes — we specialise in corporate offsites, team retreats, and MICE travel across India. We handle everything from venue scouting and group transfers to team activities and event management. Contact our B2B desk directly for a quote.
What is the best time to visit Ladakh or Spiti?
Ladakh is best from June to September when all passes are open and skies are clear. Spiti is accessible May to October. Winter Spiti (Nov–Feb) is for hardcore adventurers only. We factor in your travel dates and recommend the ideal window for your itinerary.
